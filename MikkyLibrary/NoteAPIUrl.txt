Format chung cho get 1 cái /Mikky/[tên dtb]?[tên column id của nó]=[id]
		
Trường hợp đặc biệt: 	/Mikky/Customer?Username= &Password=
			/Mikky/Admin?Username= &Password=
			/Mikky/Drink?Drinkname=
-Drink: Get all, get 1 cái, put, post, delete
-Customer: Get all, get 1 cái, put, post, delete
-Order: Get all, get 1 cái, put, post, delete
-Order detail: Get all, get 1 cái, put, post, delete
-Drink Category: Get all, get 1 cái
-Admin: Get all, get 1 cái
